Text Message Thread — Sarah & Mom

Do you ever feel like you did everything at home?
Of course. Your father worked long hours, so I handled the rest. That’s how it was.
Yeah, but I work full-time too. And I still end up making dinner, managing school stuff, cleaning, everything.
That’s just motherhood, sweetheart. It never ends.
I’m starting to resent it. I’m tired all the time. David says I’m just “better at this stuff,” but he doesn’t even try.
He folds laundry, right? That’s something.
I know he does work too but maybe I shouldn’t be doing more all the time
That’s how it goes. You’re the glue. You keep things running. You’ve always been strong. You get that from me.
Maybe that’s not a good thing. Maybe we shouldn’t have to be.
It’s just how life works. I never complained, and everything turned out fine.
But did it? Or did you just get used to being tired?
